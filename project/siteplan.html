Website Planning Document

Site Name

Seko's Flowers – A floral business offering flowers for funerals, weddings, parties, and special events.
Chosen because it’s simple, personal, and memorable.

Optional Domain: sekosflowers.com

Site Purpose

This website will showcase floral designs, allow customers to place and customize orders, and provide contact information for inquiries and bookings.

Scenarios

  • What flowers and designs are available for weddings and funerals?
  • How do I place an order and request a custom design?

Color Schema

Colors chosen for branding and consistency:

  • Deep Green (#2a5d34) – Used for headers, accents, and navigation.
  • White (#ffffff) – Used for backgrounds and text areas.

Typography

Fonts to be used:

  • Playfair Display – for headings and logo.
  • Open Sans – for body text and descriptions.
